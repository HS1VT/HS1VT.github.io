What Is Data Science and Aritifical intelligence?

Data Science brings together the best ideas from mathematics, statistics, computing, machine learning to design data driven solutions to problems spanning a wide range of application domains. Artificial intelligence is study of "intelligent agents" that sense and interact with their environment to maximize the chance of achieving their "goal".




Why Data Science and Artificial Intelligence?

Together, these two fields have the potential to transform healthcare, businesses, industry, governance, education and society.




Why B.Tech in Data Science?

The program offers strong preparation in statistical modeling, machine learning, optimization, artificial intelligence, management and analysis of massive data sets, data acquisition and data .visualization. The program focuses on topics such as reproducible data analysis, collaborative problem solving, visualization and communication, and security and ethical issues that arise in data science.
